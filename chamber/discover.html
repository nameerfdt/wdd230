History

Folsom was incorporated in 1946. FOlsom has connections to the Gold Rush, the Pony Expressm the first railroad in the west and more. Enjoy a taste of histroy in Folsom's Historic District, where you'll find premier dining, shopping, and historic architecture.

Recreational Activities

Folsom is Distinctive by Nature. Enjoy more than 50 miles of paved recreational trails, Folsom Lake, Lake Natoma, and the serene American River. Mountatin biking, hiking, and paddleboarding are just a few of the popular activities of Folsom.

Arts and Culture

The Folson community participates in many arts and cultural programs and events. The Harris Center provides enrichment through a beautiful and state-of-the-art 80,000 square foot reginal arts center. Programs are avialable at the Folsom Art Cenbter and The Gallery at 48 Natoma. You'll also notice public art all around the city.
