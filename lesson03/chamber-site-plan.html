WDD 230: Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Prison Blues Chamber of Commerce

Prison Blues is the ficticious name I've selected to name the Chamber of Commerce for the city in I live, Folsom. The chamber and it's members promote local businesses and city tourist attractions to help bring visitors to town.

Site Purpose

The Prison Blues Chamber of Commerce serves to promote local businesses and highlight the ciy's historic history. Visitors are attracted to our city by the chambers promoting local events which help foster a thriving community and drive economic growth.

Scenarios

  1. What points of interest and events will I find in the City of Prison Blues?
  2. What businesses and organizations are members of the Chamber of Commerce?
  3. What are the benefits of joining the Chamber of Commerce?
