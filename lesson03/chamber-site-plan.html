Site Name

Prison Blues Chamber of Commerce

My site name is Prison Blues Chamber of Commerce after the ficticious city for which it represents.

Site Purpose

The Prison Blues Chamber of Commerce serves to promote local businesses and highlight the city's historic history. The chamber will promote local events to bring together community members and local busiessmember to drive economic growth.

Scenarios

  • What points of interest and events will are found in the City of Prison Blues?
  • What businesses and organizations are members of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the benefits of joining the Chamber of Commerce?
